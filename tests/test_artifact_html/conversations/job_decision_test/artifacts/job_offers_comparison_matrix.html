Considering Your Family

This decision involves more than just salary and job title. Think about what your family needs right now. The startup's remote flexibility and better work-life balance could be incredibly valuable for spending time with your kids, even with a lower base salary. On the other hand, the stability and higher income from Google could offer a different kind of security, though the relocation and demanding hours present significant challenges for family life. The potential upside of startup equity is also a factor to weigh against the guaranteed higher pay of Google.
