Strategies That Seem to Help

2. Shared Reading: Reading together seems more effective than alone.

3. Effort Praise: Focusing on effort rather than just results.

4. Breaks: Taking breaks when frustration arises.

5. Book Choice: Friendship drama and engaging visuals (like in Dog Man) seem key.
