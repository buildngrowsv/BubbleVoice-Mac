Navigating Your Priorities

The Dilemma

Investor Call vs. Max's Game on Saturday

Both are important, leading to fear of negative outcomes.

Attend Max's Game
Prioritize Investor Call

Option 1: Attend Max's Game

Potential Outcomes:

Positive: Max feels supported, strengthened family bond, reduced personal guilt.

Considerations: Risk to investor relationship, potential missed funding opportunity, company success jeopardized.

Mitigation Strategies: - Delegate tasks at work if possible. - Reschedule investor call if feasible, or have co-founder lead. - Clearly communicate your commitment to Max.

Option 2: Prioritize Investor Call

Potential Outcomes:

Positive: Potential funding secured, company progress, reduced work-related stress.

Considerations: Max feels disappointed/unsupported, potential damage to father-son relationship, increased personal guilt.

Mitigation Strategies: - Ensure Max understands the importance and your commitment to other events. - Schedule dedicated quality time with Max before/after the game. - Have your co-founder fully briefed to lead the call if needed.

Remember, there's no single 'right' answer. Focus on making the best choice with the information you have, and communicate openly with those involved. Your feelings of fear and conflict are valid signals of how much you care about both areas.
